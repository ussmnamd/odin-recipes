The recipe for Anda Shami burger

Description:

Coming from the plains of South Asia, Anda Shami Burger is a balance between taste, health and time.

Ingredients:
  • Burger Buns
  • Eggs
  • Shami Kebabs
  • Salad
  • Sauces
Steps to follow:
  1. Lightly toast the burger buns
  2. Cook an omelete
  3. Cook the shami kebabs
  4. Dress the buns with salad and sauces
  5. Finally add the shami and eggs to the burger buns

Bon Apetit!
