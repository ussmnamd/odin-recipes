The recipe for Lemon Soda

Description

Ever been in absolute tropical heat? This is the medicine for all things hot and warm

Ingredients
  • Lemons
  • Carbonated/Sparkling Water
  • Mint Leafs
  • sugar
Steps to follow:
  1. Put some sugar in the carbonated water
  2. Squeeze the Lemons
  3. Put in the mint leaves for added freshness

Bon Apetit!
